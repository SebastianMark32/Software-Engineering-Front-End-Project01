Quantitative Resource Center (QRC)

The Quantitative Resource Center (QRC) is dedicated to aiding the New College community in working with quantitative matters. It provides individual and small group peer tutoring for students in Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, Computer Science, Economics, and Biology. Peer tutoring services also support students needing assistance with various quantitative methods (e.g. basic mathematics and statistics, R, SAS, SPSS, Excel, and other applications).
